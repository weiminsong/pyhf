Use and Citations¶

Citation¶

The preferred BibTeX entry for citation of pyhf is

@software{pyhf,
  author = "{Heinrich, Lukas and Feickert, Matthew and Stark, Giordon}",
  title = "{pyhf: v0.4.1}",
  version = {0.4.1},
  doi = {10.5281/zenodo.1169739},
  url = {https://github.com/scikit-hep/pyhf},
}
